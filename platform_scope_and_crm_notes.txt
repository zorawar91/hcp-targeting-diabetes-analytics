PLATFORM SCOPE & PRODUCTION CRM NOTES
HCP Targeting & Commercial Intelligence — Diabetes Portfolio
======================================================

What this prototype demonstrates vs. a production Salesforce / Veeva deployment


IMPLEMENTED IN THIS PROTOTYPE
------------------------------
- Composite HCP targeting score (Volume + Growth + Payment)
- NTILE(10) specialty-normalised decile ranking
- 4-tier segmentation: High Value / Growth / Maintenance / Deprioritise
- Rule-based Next Best Action (NBA) per HCP
- Loyalty tier model: Loyalist / Intermittent / Tourist / Non-Rx
- Call cadence engine by segment (4–24 week cycles)
- Role-based planning views: Rep / Area Manager / Regional Manager / Head of Sales
- KOL identification via CMS Open Payments data
- Territory intelligence — state-level choropleth map
- At-Risk and Breakthrough alert detection
- Silent Patient Pool Finder (moved to separate project)
- Diagnostic Gap Index per state (CDC prevalence vs actual Rx density)
- OTC-proxy HCP identification (primary care, low Rx, high-T2D states)


IN A PRODUCTION CRM DEPLOYMENT (Salesforce Health Cloud / Veeva CRM)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Account hierarchy: HCPs modelled as Contacts under Accounts (practice / hospital / health system)
- Territory Management 2.0: automated HCP-to-rep routing, no manual state filter needed
- Activity sync: every call, sample drop and email logged against the HCP record in real time
- Einstein Next Best Action: ML-driven NBA on channel, message and timing — not rule-based
- Journey stages: Unaware → Aware → Trialing → Committed → Advocate progression per HCP
- Push alerts: overdue and at-risk notifications via Slack, email, mobile
- Closed-loop measurement: Rx impact of calls (contacted vs uncontacted HCP cohort lift)
- Veeva CLM: approved email, e-detailing and sample management integration
- CRM-native call logging: reps log outcomes (reached / no see / left sample) after each visit
- Segmentation refresh: automated monthly re-scoring as new Rx data arrives


DATA NOTES
----------
- Call activity data in this prototype is SIMULATED — seeded deterministically by NPI
  for reproducibility across sessions. No real call history exists in the dataset.
- Cadence logic, loyalty tiers and segmentation rules mirror commercial pharma CRM methodology.
- NBA recommendations are rule-based (not ML). In Salesforce Health Cloud + Einstein,
  these would be model-scored and personalised per HCP interaction history.
- Prescription data: CMS Medicare Part D 2021–2022 (public dataset)
- Payment data: CMS Open Payments 2021–2022 (public dataset)
- HCP registry: NPPES National Provider Identifier database (public dataset)
- Total records: ~227K HCPs, 83M+ prescription rows across diabetes drug classes


DRUG CLASSES TRACKED
--------------------
- GLP-1 Agonists (semaglutide, liraglutide, dulaglutide, tirzepatide, exenatide)
- SGLT2 Inhibitors (empagliflozin, dapagliflozin, canagliflozin, ertugliflozin)
- DPP-4 Inhibitors (sitagliptin, saxagliptin, linagliptin, alogliptin)
- Biguanides / Metformin (metformin HCl, metformin HCl ER)
- Insulins (glargine, aspart, lispro, degludec, detemir and biosimilars)


TARGETING SCORE FORMULA
-----------------------
Score = (volume_decile / 10 × 0.40)
      + (growth_decile  / 10 × 0.40)
      + (payment_score_normalised × 0.20)

Range: 0.0 (lowest) to 1.0 (highest)

Segment assignment:
- High Value:    volume_decile >= 8 AND growth_decile >= 8
- Growth:        growth_decile >= 8 AND volume_decile < 8
- Maintenance:   volume_decile >= 8 AND growth_decile < 8
- Deprioritise:  all others
